Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account or make a purchase
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact us through our website or customer service
  • Participate in surveys or promotions
  • Leave product reviews or testimonials

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, billing and shipping addresses, payment information, and any messages or feedback you provide.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

  • IP address and geographical location
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website
  • Device information (mobile, tablet, desktop)

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you (such as payment processors, shipping companies, and email service providers).

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or other legal process, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to government requests.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL Encryption: All sensitive information is encrypted during transmission using SSL technology
  • Secure Servers: Personal information is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security measures and software
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to personal information
  • Data Minimization: We collect and retain only the information necessary for our stated purposes

Important: While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect information about how you use our website.

Essential Cookies

Necessary for basic website functionality, such as shopping cart operation and user authentication.

Analytics Cookies

Your Privacy Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access: You can request information about the personal data we have about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Restriction: You can request restriction of processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
